AI Football Commentary

Number of classes: 35, which includes all the aspects of a football game.

The link to the dataset can be found in Roboflow. The model was trained on over 6000 images.

The dataset is self-created and self-annotated because the quality of open-source data was extremely poor.

We used YOLOv8 for raw video annotation to make it easier for GPT-4 Vision to commentate on the video with the help of the annotations from YOLOv8, providing rich context for the video.

YOLOv8 was trained on 35 classes, and the results are impressive. The model's mAP performance is 86.8%, with a precision of 85.7% and a recall of 82.9%, which is excellent for a 35-class model. At the time, there were no other models that performed as well as ours. It's unclear if such models exist now.
